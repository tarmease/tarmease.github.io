Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tarmease ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate the Tarmease chatbot and associated services (the "Service"), accessible via the Facebook Messenger Platform and our website at https://www.tarmease.com.

We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that data. This policy applies to all information collected through our chatbot, website, and any related services, sales, marketing, or events.

Important Notice Regarding Facebook Messenger:

When you interact with Tarmease via Facebook Messenger, you are also subject to Meta Platforms, Inc.'s ("Meta") Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. We process data sent to us by Meta to provide our conversational service to you.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that allows us to operate the Tarmease chatbot, facilitate interactive experiences, and provide customer support.

2.1 Information Provided by Meta (Facebook)

When you initiate a conversation with us on Facebook Messenger, Meta provides us with specific information through their API:

  • Page Scoped ID (PSID): A unique identifier assigned to your conversation. It does not provide direct access to your public profile URL.
  • Public Profile Information: Fields such as first name, last name, profile picture, locale, time zone, and gender, strictly to personalize communication.
  • Message Content: The text, images, audio, attachments, and quick-reply selections you send us.

2.2 Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Details: Email, phone number, or address provided for shipping or support.
  • Transaction Data: Details regarding bookings or orders.
  • Customer Service Data: Inquiries, complaints, or feedback.

2.3 Automatically Collected Technical Data

We and our providers (like Cloudflare) may collect IP addresses, browser types, timestamps, and interaction metrics for security and debugging.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Facilitate the Chatbot Service: Using PSID and messages to generate responses and maintain context.
  • To Provide Customer Support: Resolving issues and answering inquiries.
  • To Send Administrative Information: Notifications about accounts or policy changes.
  • To Enforce Terms and Policies: Preventing abuse and ensuring platform compliance.
  • To Improve Our Services: Analyzing aggregated data to optimize accuracy.

Restrictions on Advertising: We do not sell your personal data. We do not use Facebook Messenger API data for direct marketing without explicit consent.

4. How We Share Your Information

  • Service Providers: Vendors like Cloudflare (hosting/security) and Google (data management) who process data under strict instructions.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with laws, court orders, or subpoenas.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, sales, or acquisitions.

5. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the US and potentially other countries. If you access our services from outside the US (including the EEA/UK), note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws. We implement safeguards like Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

6. Data Retention

  • Conversation Logs: Retained for up to 12 months for context and support, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Technical Logs: Retained for 30 days for security auditing.

7. Data Security

Measures include encryption at rest (AES-256), encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), and strict access controls for personnel.

8. Your Privacy Rights

8.1 EEA/UK Users (GDPR)

Rights include access, rectification, erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"), restriction, objection, and portability. Contact privacy@tarmease.com to exercise these rights.

8.2 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

Rights include knowing what data is collected, requesting deletion, and opting out of sales (we do not sell data). You also have rights under the "Shine the Light" law.

8.3 Facebook Platform Controls

You can block/mute our bot in Messenger or revoke permissions in your Facebook Settings & Privacy.

9. Cookie Policy

We use essential and analytics cookies on our website. You can opt-out of analytics cookies via browser settings. Note that Meta governs cookies within the Messenger interface.

10. Do-Not-Track Features

We do not currently respond to DNT browser signals as no uniform standard exists.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via prominent notice or chatbot notification.
